Weeks1-2

Getting started

You will have the opportunity to get to know your Cohort, meet with key senior people within the Council, find out more about the different Directorates and ensure that you are job ready. At the end of the first two weeks, you will be assigned to a project and be given a buddy and a mentor.

Months1-5

Developing confidence

This will be your opportunity to work with your line manager to find out about the objectives for the project you have been assigned, learn about the service and get stuck into making a difference. You will have a network of people there to support you, from the Graduate Team, your line manager, your buddy and your mentor.

Months6-12

Organisational awareness

This is when the Graduate Development Programme sessions start with opportunities to explore the wide strategic issues facing the City of Leeds. Working with fellow graduates, you will be able to development with the Graduate programme competencies, share ideas and proposals around real, live issues.This is the time when your first project will be coming to an end, you will review and reflect on what you have learnt and what you need next to help you develop further skills and gain different experiences. Throughout the programme, you will also have the support of a Careers Co-ordinator who will work with you to ensure that you are getting a great experience.

Months12-18

Maximising potential

You will now be in your second or even third placement and have a much better understanding of working for Leeds City Council. You will have undertaken a number of reviews around your development and you should be starting to think about where you would like to work on a permanent contract. You should continue to attend the development programme events and seek as many opportunities as possible to develop your network and raise your profile across the Council.

Months18-24

Preparing for next steps

You will be in your final project placement and working with a careers coach to find your next role. Support will be given on finding a suitable role including the completion of application forms and interview techniques.
